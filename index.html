Schedule Options Guide

×
Option 1: Rotating Days Off

In this schedule, your days off change every week. You will alternate between having Thursday/Friday off and Saturday/Sunday off throughout the month.

Pros:
  • Fairness: Everyone gets to enjoy full weekends (Saturday & Sunday) twice a month.
  • Variety: The schedule changes weekly, breaking the routine.
  • Equality: No one is "stuck" with weekday breaks.
Cons:
  • Less Stability: Harder to commit to a fixed weekly activity.
  • Changing Routine:pattern shifts every week.
Option 2: Fixed Days Off

In this schedule, your days off remain the same for the entire month. Two members will always be off on Thursday/Friday, and the other two will always be off on Saturday/Sunday.

Pros:
  • High Stability: Perfect for planning. You know exactly when you are free every week.
  • Safe Transitions: Uses a split shift to ensure no "Zombie Shifts" (working morning after night).
Cons:
  • Rigidity: If you are assigned Thu/Fri off, you never get a Sat/Sun weekend.
  • Potential Imbalance: Some members might prefer the Sat/Sun slot over Thu/Fri.
